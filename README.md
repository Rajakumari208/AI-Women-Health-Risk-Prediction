# AI-Based Women's Health Risk Prediction Platform

## Problem Statement
Women often face delayed detection of menstrual and pregnancy-related health risks due to limited access to continuous monitoring and early clinical support.

## Solution
An AI-driven platform that analyzes menstrual cycle patterns, pregnancy indicators, and lifestyle data to provide early risk prediction with explainable insights and telemedicine support.

## Key Features
- Menstrual & pregnancy health tracking
- AI-based early risk prediction
- Explainable AI insights
- Secure health data management
- Telemedicine integration
